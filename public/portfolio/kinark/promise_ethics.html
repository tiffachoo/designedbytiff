Ethnics and Accountability

The Ethical Practice and Financial Accountability Policy has been developed to ensure the integrity, transparency and accountability of all the Foundation's activities and the truthfulness of its communications in order to maintain a high level of trust by current and prospective donors, the general public, government and regulatory bodies.

To read our Ethical Practice and Financial Accountability Policy, please click here to download the PDF (36 kb).

The Kinark Foundation supports organizations that offer proven solutions and innovative practices that provide hope and help to children, youth, and families struggling with mental health issues.
