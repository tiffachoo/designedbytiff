About GSC

The club was founded in 1954, holding it's first meeting on the second floor of Galt City Hall. The old Dixon Dairy Stables in Galt served as the Clubs' indoor range. In 1957, the Club purchased the Grimer farm, a 100 acre site with a pond and a stream located on Gore Rd. Since then, another 150 acres have been added.

Membership
Information

If you are unable to join us on Wednesdays or Sundays and require more information about the club and its sections, please send us an email at moreinfo@galtsportsmensclub.com or call us at 519.621.4104. Memberships will not be accepted via email. Please bring your valid Canadian Possession & Acquisition License (PAL) with you.

Safety Policy

Possession & Acquisition License (PAL) requires completing the Canadian Firearms Safety Course (CFSC) or passing a written challenge exam. For more information see the RCMP Canadian firearms program website. www.gunexams.com has sample exams.

For firearms that are classified "restricted", you will need to complete the Canadian Restricted Firearms Safety Course (CRFSC) as well.
